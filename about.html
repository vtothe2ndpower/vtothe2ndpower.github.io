BIO

It was in late 2019 that I heard the following quote from motivational speaker Brian Tracy: "If you do not love your job then get out of it like you would get out of a burning house." At the time I was unethusiastically working as a Credit Analyst - displeased that I had an Applied Mathematics degree from UC Berkeley which I felt I wasn't using. I came across a Reddit post one night written by someone who taught themselves how to code over the span of 6 months and how it changed their life completely. From then on I began dabbling with computer programming during my lunch breaks and free time. It didn't take long before I decided to leave this job and teach myself how to code full-time. My undergraduate studies in math naturally attracted me to the logic puzzles and problem solving associated with code. I loved most that I was challenged constantly, and that there was always something new to learn next.
